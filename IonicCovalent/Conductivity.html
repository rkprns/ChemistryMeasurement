Conductivity/b>

Conductivity: is the ability of a substance to conduct electricity. In order to conduct electricty, there must be ions in the water. Ionic compounds dissolve in water a produce ions; covalent compounds do not. If a liquid conduct electricty, current can run through it and light up a bulb.
