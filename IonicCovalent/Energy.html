Energy

Energy: When bonds form, energy is released. Covalent and Ionic bond formations release different amounts of energy. You are going to observe an ionic bond forming and a covalent bond forming. For the ionic bond, we will be placing a strip of magnesium in a flame. This will create MgO. For the covalent bond, we will be placing a strip of carbon in a flame to create CO2. After watching the two videos, write down your observations and answer the quesitons in your notes.
