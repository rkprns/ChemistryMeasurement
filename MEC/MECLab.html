Mixtures

Stage 1: Below is a virtual lab for measuring mixtures. In chemistry, we generally speak of mixtures in regards to liquids, mixtures can be a combination of any state of matter. The first step in completing this lab is to read the directions. Your job is to separated the mixture into iron fillings, sand, and salt. You will need to separate the materials in a certain order in order to successfully separate the mixture. Using the equipment manual and material facts file, separate the mixture, fill out table 1, and answer the questions in your lab report

Stage 2: When you are finished with stage 1, click on the stage 2 tab to move to the next part of the lab. In this stage you will need to identify whether an unknown material is an element, compound, or mixture. If you hover over the unknown materials, you will see a discription of the material that will help you evaluate which of the three the material is. When you have successfully identified the materials, click the material disposal form to help you fill out table 2 in your notes.
