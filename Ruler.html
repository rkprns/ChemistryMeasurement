Meter Stick

Meter sticks are a way to measure length of an object. A meter stick measures things using the metric system and one meter is approximately 3.28 feet. In chemistry, we will mostly be working with the metric system (meters and grams) as opposed to using US Customary units (feet and pounds). For smaller objects, a ruler may be used. Rulers tend to have both metric and US customary units labeled. When measuring length with any tool, we tend to start at "1" as the cut of the stick can be inaccurate.
