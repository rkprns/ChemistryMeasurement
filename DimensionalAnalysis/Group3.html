Heartbeat Dimensional Analysis

Follow the directions below. For all dimensional analysis, you must show your work:

  1. Step 1: Choose one person in your group to be the time keeper and one person as the counter. The counter should place their fingers on their wrist to find their pulse. If you are having trouble finding your pulse go to this link for direction. The time keeper should start a timer for 1 minute. When the time keeper says go, they should start the timer and the counter should counting the number of beats of their heart.
    • Question 1: How many times did your heart beat in one minute?
  2. Step 2:Disney Land's Space Mountain Roller Coaster can go 28 mph and is 90 feet long.
    • Question 2: How many times would your heart beat if you rode Space Mountain?
  3. Step 3:Think of all your equivalence statement and conversion factors.
    • Question 3: write how you determined which conversion factors to use for each equivalence statement.
  4. Step 4: Due to the excitment on a roller coaster, your heart actually beats faster. Your heart rate on a roller coaster would be your exercise heart rate. Repeat step 1 but jog or hop in place while you take your heart rate.
    • Question 4:
      • What was your heart rate while moving?
      • How many more times would your heart beat if you take into account the higher heart rate on a roller coaster?
  5. Step 5: Read this article and in question 5 summarize the article in 3-5 complete sentences. Include the following information:
    • Where did the accident take place, and on what roller coaster?
    • What was the dimensional analysis mistake ?
    • What happened as a result of that dimensional analysis error?
